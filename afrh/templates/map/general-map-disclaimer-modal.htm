Please note...

The map view does not show the following types of resources:

  1. Physical resources that do not have a permanent location (such as moveable objects)
  2. Resources in the "Recurring Resources" Character Area (curbs, sidewalks)
  3. Resources in the "Spatial Patterns" Character Area (viewsheds, topography, spatial organization)

Click "SHOW HELP" at the top of the page to find a full list of omitted resources.
