Map Contents Disclaimer

The map view does not include the following types of resources:

  1. physical resources that do not have a permanent location (such as moveable objects)
  2. sets of resources included in the "Recurring Resources" Character Area (i.e. curbs, sidewalks)
  3. resources included in the "Spatial Patterns" Character Area (viewsheds, topography, and spatial organization)
